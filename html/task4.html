🏠 Welcome to My Study Notes Website

Welcome to your one-stop destination for learning smarter, not harder! This site helps students organize their study materials, access interactive tutorials, listen to tips, and dive deeper into subjects using just basic HTML...

✨ Why This Website Exists

Students often struggle with scattered notes and materials. This project organizes them in a clean, accessible format to help you understand, not just search.

🔍 Explore These Study Areas

  • 💡 Resources:
    • Helpful Platforms - Khan Academy, Coursera, edX, NASA Climate Kids

📋 How to Use This Website

  1. Start at the Subject page
  2. Click a subject for detailed explanations, diagrams, videos, and study notes
  3. Use the Resources page for interactive learning
  4. Check often for updates
